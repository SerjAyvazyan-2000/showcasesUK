Accessibility Statement

WinThrive (https://winthrive.co.uk) is committed to making our website accessible to as many users as possible, regardless of technology or ability. This accessibility statement outlines our ongoing efforts to ensure inclusive digital experiences for all visitors.

1. Commitment to Accessibility

We are actively working to ensure our website meets internationally recognized accessibility standards such as WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) 2.1, Level AA. Our goal is to provide a welcoming and seamless experience to all users, including individuals with visual, auditory, physical, or cognitive disabilities.

2. Measures Taken

To achieve our accessibility goals, we:

  • Use semantic HTML for clear structure and screen reader compatibility
  • Provide alternative text for images where necessary
  • Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background
  • Design forms with accessible labels and instructions
  • Allow full keyboard navigation across all pages
  • Regularly test and audit for accessibility issues

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge that some third-party content or linked resources may not yet fully comply with accessibility standards. We are not responsible for the accessibility of external websites but welcome feedback.

Our website is designed to be compatible with most major assistive technologies including screen readers, voice recognition software, and browser zoom settings. If you use specific accessibility tools and experience difficulties, please contact us.
